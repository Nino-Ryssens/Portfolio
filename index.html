About

02 / Profile
Antwerp, BE

A young designer from Antwerp who treats strategy, branding and digital as one continuous craft — not separate departments.

I'm Nino — currently studying Graphic & Digital Media at AP Hogeschool in Antwerp, while working as a multi-purpose creative across brand, motion, print and photography.

My approach starts with strategy and ends with the image. I like work that earns a second look: identity systems with a point of view, films with rhythm, and layouts that hold their composure. Whether it's a year embedded in-house at Euconsulting or a self-initiated spec film, I treat every brief as an exercise in clarity first, craft always.
